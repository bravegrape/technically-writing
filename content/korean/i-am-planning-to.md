## Use case

Describe intentions or plans to do something.

## Construction

Add **(으)려고 하다** to the verb stem.

| Stem ends with | Example | Construction |
|---|---|---|
| vowel | 가다  | 가**려고 하다**  |
| consonant | 읽다 | 읽**으려고 하다** |

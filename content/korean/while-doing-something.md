## Use case

Indicate that the action in the first clause happens at the same time as the action in the second clause.

## Construction

Add **(으)면서** to the verb stem.

| Stem ends with | Example | Construction |
|---|---|---|
| vowel | 마시다  | 마시**면서**  |
| consonant | 먹다 | 먹**으면서** |
